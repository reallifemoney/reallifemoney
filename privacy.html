Privacy Policy

How Real Life Money collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: April 2026 Real Life Money · Bristol
1

Who we are

Real Life Money is a financial education business based in Bristol, UK, run by Leo Dennis. We deliver in-person investing courses for individuals and organisations.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at: hello@reallifemoney.co.uk

2

What information we collect

We only collect information that is necessary to deliver our courses and communicate with you. This may include:

  • Your name and email address when you book a course through Eventbrite or TixTree
  • Your responses to post-course surveys (via Google Forms)
  • Any information you share when you contact us directly by email
  • Testimonials you provide, where you have given permission for us to use them

We do not collect any financial information, payment details, or sensitive personal data. All payments are handled directly by Eventbrite or TixTree depending on which platform the course is listed on.

3

How we use your information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Confirm your course booking and send you joining information
  • Send course reminders and follow-up materials
  • Improve our courses based on your survey feedback
  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Let you know about future courses, if you have agreed to this
We will never sell your personal data to third parties, use it for advertising purposes, or share it with anyone outside of Real Life Money without your explicit consent.
4

Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — to fulfil your course booking and deliver the service you have paid for
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our courses and communicate relevant updates to past attendees
  • Consent — where you have explicitly agreed to receive marketing communications or for your testimonial to be used publicly
5

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Eventbrite — for course bookings and payment processing. Eventbrite Privacy Policy
  • TixTree — for course bookings and payment processing. Attendee data is used solely to deliver the service and is never sold or transferred to third parties. TixTree Terms & Privacy
  • Google Forms — for post-course surveys. Google Privacy Policy
  • Mailchimp — if you join our mailing list. Mailchimp Privacy Policy

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server log data (such as IP addresses). GitHub Privacy Policy

6

How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Booking records are kept for up to 2 years for administrative purposes
  • Survey responses are kept for up to 3 years to track course improvement over time
  • Email correspondence is kept for up to 1 year
  • If you ask to be removed from our mailing list, we will action this within 5 working days
7

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Ask us to delete your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time where consent is the basis for processing
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@reallifemoney.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

You can also contact the ICO directly at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.
8

Cookies

This website does not use any tracking cookies or analytics tools. We do not track your behaviour on this site.

If you book through Eventbrite or TixTree, those platforms may set cookies in accordance with their own cookie policies.

9

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We recommend checking back periodically if you are a regular attendee.
