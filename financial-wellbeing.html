Questions

Common questions
from employers

```
How many employees can attend each course? +
Each course group is capped at 12 people. This is intentional — small groups mean everyone can ask questions, the sessions stay interactive, and no one gets left behind. If you have more staff interested, we can run multiple course rounds.
How long does each session take and when are they delivered? +
Each session is 60 minutes, delivered weekly across three weeks. We can deliver in the working day, at lunch, or after hours — whichever works best for your team. The only space you need is a room that comfortably seats up to 12 people.
Is this regulated financial advice? +
No — and that's important. This is financial education, not regulated financial advice. No specific products are recommended, no FCA permissions are required, and there's no risk of mis-selling. Employees leave with knowledge and tools to make their own decisions. This also means it's straightforward to offer as a wellbeing benefit without any compliance headaches.
Do you need any special equipment or setup? +
Very little. A room with enough chairs, a screen or TV to connect a laptop to, and ideally WiFi so attendees can explore investment platforms in Session Three. That's it. Everything else — materials, games, activities — is provided.
What if some of my staff already invest — will they still get value? +
Yes. The course is designed for complete beginners but regularly produces insights even for people who have already started investing. Understanding risk properly, comparing funds, and knowing which tax wrappers to use are areas where even experienced investors often have gaps. Nobody has ever left saying they already knew all of it.
```
