The Complete Course Proposal

A balanced, highly practical format designed around school time constraints.

Core Pathway

Investing Course

Format: 2 × 90-minute interactive sessions – capped strictly at a maximum of 12 attendees per course to preserve engagement.

  • Session 1: Why invest, breaking down asset types, and managing individual risk tracks.
  • Session 2: Selecting account types, picking specific funds, and navigating the mechanics of clicking the invest button safely.
Bonus Integration

Teachers' Pension Session

Format: Bonus 60-minute standalone masterclass—open broadly to up to 30 members of staff simultaneously.

  • Completely accessible to all school staff—not restricted purely to core course attendees.
  • Demystifies how the Teachers' Pension Scheme works, mapping real scenarios and career projection trends.
  • Designed entirely as a practical, active walkthrough rather than a passive text lecture.
