The course

Two evenings.
A lifetime of clarity.

Each session is 90 minutes, kept to a maximum of 12 people, and packed with practical activities — not just slides.

Session One · 90 mins

Types of investment
and managing risk

The foundations of investing

  • Why we invest — and what inflation is quietly doing to your savings right now
  • The main asset classes explained simply: cash, bonds, equities and more
  • Monopoly money simulation — watch how different investments behave over time
  • Understanding risk: what it really means and how to manage it for your situation
Session Two · 90 mins

Picking your portfolio
and clicking invest

Putting it into practice

  • Get to know real funds using a card game — compare, rank and discuss
  • ISAs, pensions and investment accounts: which wrapper is right for you
  • Finding investments that suit your goals and risk appetite
  • Hands-on platform exploration — and actually clicking that invest button
Your teacher

I've seen the advice gap up close

Before becoming a maths teacher, I spent several years working as a qualified financial adviser. What I witnessed over and over again was people making costly, avoidable mistakes — not because they were careless, but because nobody had ever taught them the basics.

Financial literacy isn't taught in schools. Proper financial advice is expensive and inaccessible for most people. That gap bothered me — so I built Real Life Money to do something about it.

There's nothing to sell you here. No products, no follow-up calls, no referral fees. Just two evenings of honest, practical education from someone who has seen both sides.
